Teaching Point

Insecure Design is not just a missing if statement. The deeper problem is that the required business rule was not designed, specified, tested, or enforced.

Bad Design

total = price * quantity
accept_order(total)

Better Design

require quantity >= 1
require total >= 0
then accept_order(total)
